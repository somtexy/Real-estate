About This Property

Discover refined living in this beautifully designed luxury apartment situated in the prestigious Maitama district of Abuja. Every detail has been carefully considered, from the premium marble flooring and expansive living spaces to the elegant lighting and floor-to-ceiling windows that flood the home with natural light.

The residence features a spacious open-plan living room, a fully fitted contemporary kitchen with high-end appliances, generously sized ensuite bedrooms, luxurious bathrooms, ample wardrobe space, and private balconies offering panoramic city views.

Residents enjoy access to excellent infrastructure, uninterrupted power supply, treated water, high-speed internet readiness, 24-hour security, CCTV surveillance, dedicated parking, and close proximity to restaurants, shopping malls, business districts, hospitals, and international schools.

Whether you're searching for your next family home or a premium investment opportunity, this property offers an exceptional blend of comfort, security, convenience, and long-term value.
